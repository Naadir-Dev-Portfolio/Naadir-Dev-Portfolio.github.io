About

I am passionate about building things that actually work — not just technically, but for the people who depend on them every day. Seven years of enterprise experience across MI reporting, senior development, and data & technology leadership have sharpened my instinct for what matters: clean automation, well-structured data pipelines, reliable reporting, and tools that save real time. Mostly self-taught across Python, VBA, Power BI, React Native, and AI, I bring a maker's curiosity and a no-limits mindset to every project. I also understand that speed without governance is a liability — responsible automation means building pipelines and systems that are compliant, auditable, and accountable. In an era where AI is increasingly embedded in business workflows, the human understanding of governance, data integrity, and compliance standards is more critical than ever. Whether it's a Python pipeline that eliminates hours of manual effort or an AI-powered dashboard that turns raw data into decisions, I build things that last and that organisations can trust.
